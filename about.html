Why does this exist?

During this month at Full Sail University we will be taking our first steps to creating a Website. The request was originally to make a simple HTML page with "Hello World". It drove me nuts how boring it looked so without jumping too far ahead I created this site to build off of in the future. I hope everyone is as excited to see the evolution of this page as I am!

Who am I?

My name is Daniel Schmelzer and after close to 15 years in the restaurant industry mustered the courage to pursue a long time dream. I enrolled at Full Sail University to help me attain the knowledge to become a successful Web Developer. Over the last year we were challenged with learning C# and once we proved ourselves worthy we were were given passage to HTML, CSS and JavaScript where I currently stand in the program. As we progress though the program I will add more and really showcase my creativity.
